Our Story

Where every thread
tells a story of craft.

M'PALATH was born from a deep reverence for the art of tailoring — where couture is not just clothing, but a ceremony of self-expression. Each piece is conceived to honour the woman who wears it.

Founded by Devapriya Renjith, our atelier in Kochi, India operates on an intimate, appointment-only model. Every garment passes through thirty hand-finishing stages before it leaves our studio.

A newly launched creative studio focused on modern design and high-quality experiences.

Quick Answers

M'PALATH Atelier FAQ

M'PALATH is a designer boutique in Kochi for women looking for bridal couture, bespoke tailoring, occasion wear, trousseau curation, and careful garment restoration with a private fitting process.

What is M'PALATH?

M'PALATH is an appointment-only luxury atelier and designer boutique in Kochi, Kerala, founded by Devapriya Renjith.

Does M'PALATH make bridal couture in Kochi?

Yes. The studio creates custom bridal couture, wedding outfits, reception looks, and trousseau pieces through private consultations and fittings.

Can I book bespoke tailoring at M'PALATH?

Yes. Bespoke tailoring includes design direction, fabric selection, silhouette development, toile fittings, and final finishing for garments made only for the client.

How It Works

Our Atelier Process

01

Discovery Call

We begin with a private consultation to understand your vision, aesthetic, and occasion. No garment is started without deep listening.

02

Design & Fabric

Mood boards, silhouette sketches, and fabric selection from our curated, ethically-sourced library of textiles.

03

Toile Fittings

A muslin prototype is crafted first. Two to three fittings refine the drape and structure until the silhouette is flawless.

04

Final Delivery

The finished garment arrives wrapped in tissue, with a hand-written care card and a care consultation included.
